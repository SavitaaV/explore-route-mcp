VOICEOVER PRODUCTION NOTES
Since Replit is generating the voice, keep every sentence under 12 words. Short declarative sentences read better through TTS. The script above is written for this — do not add words between the sentences I've written.
Total word count for audio: approximately 195 words across 75 seconds. That's 2.6 words per second — comfortable TTS pace.
